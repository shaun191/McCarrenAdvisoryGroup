Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how McCarren Advisory Group, LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you visit www.mccarrenadvisory.com, contact us, or schedule a call. By using our website, you agree to this Policy.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide

When you contact us, request a call, or complete an intake form, you may provide your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any details you choose to include in your message.

Scheduling information

We use Microsoft Bookings / Outlook to schedule introductory calls. Information you submit when booking is processed by Microsoft under its own privacy terms, in addition to this Policy.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the site, we and our analytics providers automatically collect usage information through cookies and similar technologies — for example, the pages you view, links and buttons you click, scrolling and mouse activity, referring pages, and general device, browser, and approximate location (derived from IP address) data.

2. Cookies and analytics tools

We use the following third-party tools, which set cookies or use similar technologies:

  • PostHog — product analytics and heatmaps. Captures page views, clicks, scroll and interaction data, device/browser information, and approximate location, to help us understand how the site is used and improve it. Our PostHog data is hosted in the United States.
  • Google Ads (gtag.js) — advertising and conversion measurement. Helps us measure the performance of our ads (for example, when a visit leads to a booking) and may support remarketing.

Cookies generally fall into three categories: strictly necessary, analytics, and advertising. Most browsers let you block or delete cookies through their settings.

3. How we use information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide requested information or services.
  • To schedule and conduct introductory calls and engagements.
  • To operate, maintain, analyze, and improve our website.
  • To measure and improve our marketing and advertising.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as follows:

  • Service providers/processors that operate our tools on our behalf — including PostHog (analytics), Google (advertising/measurement), and Microsoft (scheduling and email).
  • Legal and safety — when required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.

  • Cookies: adjust or disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Advertising: manage Google ad personalization in your Google Account ad settings, and opt out of interest-based ads via industry tools (e.g., optout.aboutads.info).
  • Depending on where you live (for example, California under the CCPA/CPRA, or the EU/UK under GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your personal information, and to opt out of certain sharing. To exercise any right, email us at preparedwith@mccarrenadvisory.com.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.
